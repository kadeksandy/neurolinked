AI-Powered Music Composition Tool

by Sarah Chen • 3 days ago

I envision creating an AI-powered tool that helps musicians and composers generate unique melodies and harmonies. The tool would analyze existing musical patterns and create original compositions based on user preferences for genre, mood, and instrumentation.

This could revolutionize how we approach music creation, making it accessible to people without formal musical training while still providing powerful tools for professional composers. I'm looking for collaborators with experience in machine learning, music theory, and UI/UX design.
