FAQs

All You Need to Know About NEUROLINKED

We've gathered answers to help you better understand how to share ideas, join projects, and explore innovation through our AI-powered platform.

What is NEUROLINKED and how does it work?

NEUROLINKED is a digital platform where individuals can submit aspirations, discover others with similar visions, and collaborate on future-driven projects using the power of AI.

How do I publish my idea or aspiration?

Just go to the “Write Aspiration” section, fill out your vision using our form, and submit. Our AI system may even help connect your idea with potential collaborators.

Is using NEUROLINKED free?

Yes! NEUROLINKED is free to use. Anyone can share aspirations, view projects, and connect with collaborators without any subscription.

Can I join a project even if I didn’t start it?

Absolutely. You can browse open projects or use our AI matcher to find initiatives that need your skills or align with your interests.

How is my data and idea protected?

All submissions and accounts are secured using encryption. We prioritize your privacy and never share your ideas without your permission.
